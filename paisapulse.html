Welcome to PaisaPulse

Let's make your money data permanent.

Your expenses will live in a Google Sheet inside your own Google Drive — a free, private database only you can see. No sign-in, no API keys, nothing for me to host.

1

Create your Sheet

Open a fresh Google Sheet — this becomes your private database. Name it anything (e.g. "PaisaPulse Data"). Leave it open for step 2.

2

Add the script & deploy it

A tiny script turns your Sheet into a private API. In your Sheet open Extensions → Apps Script, delete the sample, paste the script below, and Save.

  1. In your Sheet: Extensions → Apps Script
  2. Delete the sample code, paste the script above, click Save 💾
  3. Deploy → New deployment → select type Web app
  4. Execute as Me, Who has access Anyone → Deploy
  5. Authorize when prompted, then copy the Web app URL (ends in /exec)
